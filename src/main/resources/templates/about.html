What is Get a Pet?

Get A Pet is a website that focuses on adopting pets. You can choose from cats, dogs, rabbits, parrots to everything you can imagine.


Besides the adoption, we offer other services too. You can sit a pet or walk a pet. If you are going on a vacation, you can find someone to sit your pet!
